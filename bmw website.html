Founders

A common question is whether or not Harley-Davidson is a person. It’s actually four people. William Harley was only a year older than Arthur Davidson. The two boys grew up just a couple of houses apart in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Such was the start of their lifelong friendship. Both boys were enamored with bicycles.
